# Mission, Vision & Values

These are the company's north stars — referenced in hiring, product trade-offs, partner choices, and pricing decisions. Short by design.

## Mission

**Make professional services transparent for the people paying for them.**

Every project a firm runs has a client on the other side. Today, that client is in the dark. Siapp's job is to close that gap — automatically — so trust replaces escalation.

## Vision (5 years)

**The default way firms in Southeast Asia run client-facing projects.**

When a homeowner in Penang hires a contractor, a couple in Johor Bahru hires a conveyancer, or a startup in Jakarta hires a designer — they expect to receive a Siapp link the way they expect a tracking number from a courier.

## Values (operating principles)

We pick values that **change behavior under pressure**, not posters. Each comes with a "we will / we won't."

### 1. Client trust is the product

- **We will** treat the client portal as a first-class surface, not a feature.
- **We won't** ship anything to the firm side that creates a bad client-side experience, even if it's faster to build.

### 2. Templates over toolkits

- **We will** encode opinionated workflows that get a firm productive in an hour.
- **We won't** add another empty canvas and call it flexibility.

### 3. Local-first, not localized

- **We will** design for WhatsApp, BM, MYR, FPX, local industry norms from day 1.
- **We won't** ship an obviously-US product and patch translations later.

### 4. Show the work

- **We will** publish honest case studies with real numbers — wins and losses.
- **We won't** pretend a pilot is a customer or a feature is shipped before it is.

### 5. Discipline beats scope

- **We will** ship less, better, and on time.
- **We won't** chase features outside the wedge to win a single deal.

### 6. Be in the room

- **We will** spend monthly time on-site with at least one firm (job site, law office).
- **We won't** decide roadmap from Slack and dashboards alone.

### 7. Compound, don't burn

- **We will** prioritize loops that get cheaper over time (PLG client loop, template marketplace, referrals).
- **We won't** rent growth we can't sustain.

## What we are not

To stay honest:

- We are **not** a horizontal PM tool. Don't compare us to Asana.
- We are **not** a construction ERP. Don't compare us to Procore.
- We are **not** a law practice management suite. Don't compare us to Clio.
- We are **not** a WhatsApp BSP. We sit on one.
