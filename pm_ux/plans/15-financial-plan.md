# Financial Plan & Funding

Numbers below are **directional** — placeholders for the model to refine with quotes and discovery data. The point is the *shape* of the plan and the funding decision.

## Funding stance

**Bias to bootstrap, raise opportunistically.** Two warm design partners + an SMB SaaS price point + low infra costs makes capital-efficient growth plausible.

| Stance | When to take it |
|---|---|
| **Bootstrap** + grants (default) | Phase 0–1 with founder savings + design partner revenue + MDEC/Cradle grants |
| **Pre-seed (RM 1.5–3M)** | At Phase 1 → 2 transition if metrics earn it and a market window opens (e.g. competitor enters MY) |
| **Seed (USD 1.5–3M)** | At Phase 2 → 3 transition, to fund SEA expansion |
| **Series A** | After SEA expansion proven; only if growth outpaces what cashflow can fund |

Reasons to lean bootstrap longer:

- Honest SMB pricing → predictable revenue.
- Founders keep control of roadmap and pace.
- Avoid the "must be unicorn" pressure that breaks vertical/local plays.

Reasons we might raise earlier:

- Window of competitive entry into MY (e.g. Buildertrend pricing pivot).
- Channel partnership that needs upfront commitment.
- Hiring a senior engineering lead requires equity that's easier to package with cash.

## Sources of non-dilutive capital (MY-specific)

- **Cradle Fund — CIP Spark / CIP Sprint** — early-stage grants
- **MDEC programs** — digital startup grants, market access programs
- **MyDigital initiatives** — digitalization grants subsidize SaaS adoption (customer-side, helps revenue)
- **SME Corp** — SME-focused funding
- **MAVCAP / Penjana Kapital** — equity, later stage

These take time; start applications in Phase 0.

## Runway scenarios

| Scenario | Inputs | Burn / mo | Required runway to launch + cushion |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lean bootstrap | 2 founders deferred, 1 engineer | ~25k MYR | 200–250k MYR (~9 months) |
| Realistic bootstrap | 1 founder partial salary, 1 engineer, 1 CS | ~45k MYR | 400–500k MYR |
| Funded pre-seed | 3 engineers, 1 design, 1 CS, 1 founder salary | ~95k MYR | 1.5M MYR (16 months) |

Pick the scenario that matches founders' personal runway tolerance. **Don't lie to yourself about deferred salary.**

## Margin model sanity check

Per **mid-tier (Business)** customer paying RM 499/mo:

| Cost | Estimate (MYR/mo) |
|---|---|
| WhatsApp BSP (included allowance, mostly utility templates) | 60–100 |
| Hosting + storage + email + monitoring (allocated) | 25 |
| Support cost (allocated, target 1 ticket/mo at 10 min) | 15 |
| Payment processing (~2.5%) | 12 |
| **COGS** | **~110–150** |
| **Gross margin** | **70–78%** |

If BSP costs run higher (chatty client, lots of marketing-category messages), margin compresses. **Mitigation:** overages on messaging, marketing templates billed at higher rate, encourage utility-categorized templates.

## Pricing leverage on profitability

A single tier upgrade (Team → Business) more than doubles per-customer gross profit. Net revenue retention > 110% is therefore plausible *without* raising headline prices.

## Reporting cadence

- **Weekly:** founder dashboard — MRR, new logos, churn, top support themes, BSP cost trend.
- **Monthly:** to whole team — finance summary, runway, customer story, roadmap status.
- **Quarterly:** to advisors / investors (when present) — formal update letter.
- **Annual:** statutory accounts + board strategy review.

## Funding decision triggers

We do not raise unless one of these is true:

1. We can prove a CAC payback < 4 months with a paid channel, and capital would buy growth (not survival).
2. A competitor is moving into MY at scale; capital lets us pre-empt.
3. Specific senior hire requires runway-backed compensation.
4. Founder runway hits 6 months and no path to positive cashflow within 9 months.

## Investor pitch positioning (when needed)

- **What:** the SEA-native, vertical-templated, WhatsApp-first project tracker.
- **Wedge:** construction + legal in MY, expanding to ID + SG.
- **Distribution loop:** every project surfaces our brand to N clients; some clients run firms; firms convert.
- **Margin:** SaaS-class gross margin (70%+) with usage layer.
- **Why us:** local roots, two paying design partners, real templates, BSP relationships.
- **Ask:** specific number tied to a specific 18-month outcome (not "for 18 months of runway").

## Anti-patterns we will avoid

- Raising before validating wedge ("just enough money to die slower").
- Hiring ahead of revenue + > 6 months of runway.
- Building features to please an investor who's "watching."
- Counting design-partner discounted MRR as "real" MRR in board updates.
- Hiding from the WhatsApp BSP cost curve.
